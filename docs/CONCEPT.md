# Project Concept

## Why (One Sentence)

Job Ranger enables job seekers to automatically monitor multiple company career pages, filter opportunities by personalized criteria, and receive timely alerts—all while keeping data completely local and private.

## Vibe (Three Keywords)

1. **Private** - All data stays on your machine; no cloud, no tracking, no compromise
2. **Automated** - Set it and forget it; background scraping on your schedule
3. **Efficient** - Filter noise, surface signal; see only jobs that matter to you

## Anti-Goals (What This Is NOT)

- NOT a job board aggregator (you choose specific companies to monitor)
- NOT a cloud service (no accounts, no sync, no subscription)
- NOT an auto-apply tool (discovery only, you control applications)
- NOT AI-dependent (works entirely with deterministic extraction)
- NOT a resume parser or matcher
- NOT multi-user or team-oriented

## Core Value Proposition

Job seekers waste hours manually checking career pages. Job Ranger automates this surveillance loop while respecting privacy and providing honest feedback about extraction capabilities—never pretending to work when it can't.
